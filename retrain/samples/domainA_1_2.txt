1.2 Relation to the work programme
1.2.1 Relation to the work programme
The proposal relates to the specific challenge of destination 3 of the NMBP-01-2018 call: “Advanced materials and processing for additive manufacturing”, which calls for proposals for the development of new materials and processing approaches for AM, supported by the necessary simulation tools, to overcome the current limitations of the AM technology for specific industrial applications. The proposal is within the scope of the call and aims to advance the technology readiness level of AM by addressing the challenges outlined in the call. The proposal will also contribute to the key performance indicators of the call by providing new materials and manufacturing process options for AM technologies that will enable the manufacturing of more complex parts for industrial applications and the exploitation of the potential of AM, as outlined in section 1.1.
