of these partners have already worked together with many other 
partners of this consortium in the frame of past and on -going 
research projects (Section 1.3 ). The CEM-WAVE consortium is 
composed by a strong and strategic consortium with a wide know-
how and expertise in the different areas covered in the project.

scientific and technical competencies covering all relevant fields: a)

KNEIA, which is specialized in communication and dissemination

UNIPI, and all the consortium. On the whole, the CEM-WAVE
