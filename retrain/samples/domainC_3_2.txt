=== Section 3_2: Management structure and procedures ===

2.3 Management structure and procedures  
a. Organisational structure and decision-making, including monitoring of milestones: The diagram in Figure 3.4 illustrates the organisational structure of our project. The Project Coordinator (PC) is responsible for the timely and efficient implementation of the project, as well as for its overall financial and scientific management. The PC will be the main point of contact with the EC. A Steering Committee (SC) will be established, including one representative per partner, in order to make important decisions regarding the implementation of the project and to solve any issues that may arise. The SC will be supported by a Management Team (MT) consisting of the Project Coordinator, Project Manager (PM), and Scientific Manager (SM). The PM and SM will be in charge of the day-to-day management of the project and will report directly to the PC. The PM will be responsible for the financial monitoring, while the SM will be responsible for the technical monitoring. A Project Office (PO) will be established as the main contact point for all consortium members and as the main interface between the project consortium and the EC. The PO will be responsible for the day-to-day management of the project, including the organisation of all project meetings and the preparation of all reports. Finally, the Project Technical Coordinator (PTC) will be in charge of the coordination of the technical activities. The main instrument for the monitoring of the project will be the deliverables and milestones.
