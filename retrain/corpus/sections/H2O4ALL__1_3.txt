1.3 Collaboration with national and international research H2OforAll is aligned with previous and ongoing projects to give a vision on past achievements that will help while looking to the future. Below is a small list of projects in which H2OforAll partners participate: Sustainable technology for the staged recovery of an agricultural water from high moisture fermentation products, EU-WATER JPI. Experience making on magnetic char from digestates will be used in H2OforAll. Development of Catalytic Systems for Effluents Treatment by Photoassisted oxidation, POCI-01-0247-FEDER-047545. The materials and reactors developed within this project will be applied within H2OforAll framework for DBPs and DBPs precursors removal from water by photocatalytic oxidation. How to protect water, soil and plants production all together, EU-Water JPI/006/2016. The relationship between these ecosystems and the know-how acquired with this project involving the protection of water and soil together will be crucial for H2OforAll goals achievement. MEKOROT – Environmental surveys for drinking water supply systems for several municipalities. This project will give valuable data regarding drinking water quality within the supply systems. Cost AERoGELS – Advanced Engineering and Research of (aero)Gels for Environment and Life Sciences. This Action can support dissemination/mobility stays related to the aerogels' work in H2OforAll. Aqua2S (H2020) – Enhancing standardization strategies to integrate innovative technologies for safety and security in existing water networks.
