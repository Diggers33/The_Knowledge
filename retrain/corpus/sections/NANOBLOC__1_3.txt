1.3 12/10/2022 In this version of the DoA, while keeping the original deliverables, we provide a greater 
description as to why we split some deliverables during the Contract Negotiations 
process.  
The sole reason was to ensure that any deliverable that originally (at proposal stage) had 
multiple dates of delivery, would be split into a separate deliverable for each date of 
delivery (and the deliverables were then numbered (ordered) according to the date of 
delivery).  
For example, at proposal stage, we originally had D1.3 Report on wet chemistry & 
biobased coatings to be delivered at M12 and M24. During the preparation of the DoA 
we split this deliverable in two, to ensure that each date of delivery would have its own 
Deliverable, so that each version could be uploaded in its unique placeholder in the 
electronic system. As an example: the current status in the DoA is that we have D1.3 1st 
Report on wet chemistry & biobased coatings to be delivered at M12, and D1.4 2nd 
Report on wet chemistry & biobased coatings to be delivered at M24. Likewise, we 
performed the same process for all deliverables that at proposal stage had multiple dates 
of delivery for the same deliverable.
