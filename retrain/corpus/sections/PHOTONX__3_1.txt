3.1 Work plan and resources

Brief presentation of the overall structure of the work plan

The PhotonX work plan is structured into six interlinked Work Packages (WPs) that ensure a coherent progression from sensor technology and multimodal integration platform scale-up (Track 1- WP1, WP2), through implementing multimodal integration in 3 complex industrial use cases for real-world validation (Track 2, WP4), to preparing the frameworks for post-project industrialisation, standardisation, IP protection, business scenarios and exploitation pathways for market deployment and adoption (Track 3, WP4). The design reflects both the scientific/technical ambition of the project, industrial validation, and the exploitation-driven objectives.
The workplan is also supported with a solid campaign for communication, dissemination and ecosystem networking and engagement (WP5), and robust R&I Project Management (WP6). The workplan Pert is provided in Fig 10, and the Gantt (Table 15) shows the work plan mapped over 36 months with a phased but overlapping structure that ensures continuous feedback between technical development, integration, validation, and exploitation.
