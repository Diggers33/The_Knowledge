Photon counting X-ray imaging offers some key advantages compared to the current technology in clinical use. First of all, the technique can provide spectral information, which can be used to improve the image contrast and reduce the dose delivered to the patient. Moreover, photon counting technology allows the system to record individual photon events, which enables the use of new strategies for image formation, including a direct conversion of the X-ray photons into electrical signals, molecular imaging, and the use of phase contrast techniques.
