=== Domain B: photonic sensors for pharmaceutical inline process analytical technology (PAT) ===
=== Section 2_3: Communication and open science ===

2.3.1. Communication activities | Photonic sensors for pharmaceutical inline process analytical technology (PAT)

- We will develop a fully integrated website with a dedicated page on the Horizon Results Platform to promote the project and disseminate the results.
- We will create a project logo, a project flyer, and a project video to facilitate communication.
- We will create a project Facebook page, LinkedIn group, and Twitter profile.
- We will publish articles in specialized journals and magazines.
- We will produce a newsletter to be sent to our stakeholders.
- We will conduct workshops and webinars to explain the project, its objectives, and its potential benefits.
- We will participate in exhibitions, fairs, and other meetings to disseminate the project results.
- We will organize a public event at the end of the project to disseminate the results.

2.3.2. Open science | Photonic sensors for pharmaceutical inline process analytical technology (PAT)

- We will make our publications open access.
- We will develop and disseminate data management plans (DMPs).
- We will create a project repository for the publication of data, publications, and other results.
- We will use the Horizon Results Platform to communicate the results of the project.
- We will ensure compliance with the FAIR principles when publishing data.
- We will publish the results in open access journals.
- We will store data in open-access repositories.
- We will publish the data in open data journals.
- We will develop a data management plan to increase the impact of the project and to guarantee the principles of open science.
