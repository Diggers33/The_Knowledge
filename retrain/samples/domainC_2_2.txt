=== Domain C: hyperspectral imaging for automated plastic waste sorting in circular economy facilities ===
=== Section 2_2: Dissemination, exploitation and communication ===

2.2 Communication, dissemination, exploitation, and outreach 2.2.1 Communication and dissemination strategy Our communication and dissemination plan aims to maximise the impact of our results by communicating and disseminating our results to different stakeholders, including the scientific and industrial communities, as well as to the general public.

Communication and dissemination activities for our project will be implemented as a continuous process during the project and will be regularly monitored and evaluated. In the first year of our project, a dissemination plan will be developed, which will be updated as the project progresses. The impact of the communication activities will be maximised by collaborating with the project’s Industry Advisory Board (IAB) in disseminating the results to the wider scientific and professional community. Furthermore, the IAB will assist stakeholders to access our project’s results, thus facilitating their exploitation. We will develop a communication strategy, which will be regularly updated, to guarantee the exploitation of our project’s results. An initial plan of our communication and dissemination activities is provided in Table 4.4.
