1.1.1 Objectives and ambition  

1.1.1.1 Objectives  

The objective of SmartCoat is to demonstrate the cost-efficient production of the integrated ultra-fast, ultra-bright and fully flexible POF-based LED (I-LED) coating of BOPET foils, which are of vital importance for the production of smart packaging. This objective will be achieved by the development of a novel POF coating process, which will be based on the surface activation and plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition, followed by the UV/EB curing of the POF coating on the BOPET foil. We will study the influence of the different process parameters on the properties of the coated foils, including the mechanical and optical properties and the adhesion of the POF coating to the BOPET foil. We will optimise the properties of the I-LED coated BOPET foils by the addition of fillers in the POF coating, which will be selected to increase the brightness and emission speed of the I-LEDs, as well as to improve the mechanical properties of the coated foils. To meet the cost-efficiency objective, we will study the influence of the process parameters on the production rate of the POF coated BOPET foils. We will study the practical feasibility of the developed POF coating process by performing a pilot scale production of the I-LED coated BOPET foils. We will manufacture I-LEDs from the developed coated BOPET foils and investigate their optical and mechanical properties, including their brightness and emission speed, their flexibility and their adhesion to the BOPET foil. We will assess the cost efficiency of the developed POF coating process by calculating the cost of the SmartCoat process on a laboratory and on a pilot scale and by comparing it to the costs of the state-of-the-art POF coating processes. In summary, the objectives of SmartCoat are:  
■ To develop a novel POF coating process, which will be based on the surface activation and plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition, followed by the UV/EB curing of the POF coating on the BOPET foil.  
■ To optimise the properties of the I-LED coating, including its mechanical and optical properties and its adhesion to the BOPET foil.  
■ To increase the brightness and emission speed of the I-LEDs and to improve the mechanical properties of the coated foils by the addition of fillers in the POF coating.  
■ To achieve a cost-efficient production of the I-LED coated BOPET foils by increasing the production rate of the POF coating process.  
■ To demonstrate the cost-efficient production of the integrated ultra-fast, ultra-bright and fully flexible POF-based LED (I-LED) coating of BOPET foils by performing a pilot scale production of the I-LED coated BOPET foils.  
■ To manufacture I-LEDs from the developed coated BOPET foils and investigate their optical and mechanical properties, including their brightness and emission speed, their flexibility and their adhesion to the BOPET foil.  
■ To assess the cost efficiency of the developed POF coating process by calculating the cost of the SmartCoat process on a laboratory and on a pilot scale and by comparing it to the costs of the state-of-the-art POF coating processes.
