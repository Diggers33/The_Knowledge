1.3 Concept and Approach

1.3.1. Concept and Approach

1.3.1.1. Concept and Approach of the activities

This research will be undertaken in 3 phases, as outlined in the table below:

Phase | Purpose | Process | Outcomes
:--- | :--- | :--- | :---
1 | To identify the stakeholders, challenges and opportunities | Literature reviews, interviews with stakeholders | Report on the scope of the project
2 | To develop the materials and coatings | Material synthesis | Developed and characterised materials and coatings
3 | To test the materials and coatings | Material testing | Evaluated materials and coatings

1.3.1.2. Concept and Approach of the activities

The main objective of this research is to develop graphene oxide (GO) based nanocomposite materials with antimicrobial activity for application in health care. The specific objectives are:

- To identify the stakeholders, challenges and opportunities
- To develop the materials and coatings
- To test the materials and coatings

The table below presents the research questions and the methods to be applied:

Research questions | Method | Information sought
:--- | :--- | :---
What are the current materials and coatings for antimicrobial surfaces? | Literature review | To identify the stakeholders, challenges and opportunities
What are the requirements for the materials, coatings and sensors? | Stakeholder consultation | As above
What are the best GO based materials for antimicrobial surfaces? | Material synthesis | To develop the materials and coating
What are the best GO based coatings for antimicrobial surfaces? | As above | As above
What are the best GO based materials for electrochemical biosensors? | As above | As above
What are the best GO based coatings for electrochemical biosensors? | As above | As above
What are the best GO based materials for surface plasmon resonance biosensors? | As above | As above
What are the best GO based coatings for surface plasmon resonance biosensors? | As above | As above
What are the most suitable methods for materials and coatings testing? | Literature review | To test the materials and coating
How do the materials and coatings perform? | Materials and coatings testing | As above
