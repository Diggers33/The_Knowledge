=== Domain A: NIR spectroscopy for real-time food quality control in smart manufacturing lines ===
=== Section 2_3: Communication and open science ===

2.3.2. Quality control in a smart manufacturing line (QCM) – (Innovation Action)  

Objective:  
The objective is to develop a smart manufacturing line for real-time quality control of food products (tomato, cheese, ham, and salmon) using near-infrared (NIR) spectroscopy and chemometric modelling to enable fast, non-destructive, and accurate inspection of the products to achieve a high level of food safety, quality, and traceability.  

Innovation:  
The innovation lies in the development of a smart manufacturing line for real-time quality control of food products using NIR spectroscopy and chemometric modelling to achieve a high level of food safety, quality, and traceability.  

Research/innovation activities:  
A prototype smart manufacturing line will be established for each product, comprising NIR devices, data acquisition systems, and a control system. The developed line will be integrated into the production lines of the partners to monitor the production processes and enable fast, non-destructive, and accurate inspection of the products. The consortium will work on the following:  
- Development of a smart manufacturing line for real-time quality control of food products using NIR spectroscopy and chemometric modelling.  
- Development of a control system using the data acquired from the NIR devices to monitor the production processes and enable fast, non-destructive, and accurate inspection of the products.  
- Integration of the developed line into the production lines of the partners.  
- Development of a cloud-based platform to enable the integration of the developed line into the production lines.  

Expected results:  
- Developed smart manufacturing line for real-time quality control of food products using NIR spectroscopy and chemometric modelling.  
- Developed control system using the data acquired from the NIR devices to monitor the production processes and enable fast, non-destructive, and accurate inspection of the products.  
- Developed cloud-based platform to enable the integration of the developed line into the production lines.  
- Number of publications and patents.  
- Number of products.  
- Number of SMEs.  
- Number of jobs.
