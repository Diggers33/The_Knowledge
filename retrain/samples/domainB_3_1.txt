4.1 Work plan — Work packages, deliverables
WP1: Project management and coordination
Objectives:
• To establish a sound technical and administrative project management structure.
• To guarantee the smooth technical implementation of the project.
• To establish a communication and collaboration structure for the project.
• To promote the project results among industry and academia.
• To provide the project partners with an efficient project administration system.
• To guarantee the quality of the project results and deliverables.
• To guarantee the quality of the project results and deliverables.
• To organise the project meetings and the final workshop.
Description of work (including approach, methodology and milestones)
The management structure is designed as a two-level system consisting of the General Assembly (GA) and the Executive Board (EB). The GA will be the decision-making body in the project, where all partners will be represented by their respective Project Manager. The GA will meet twice a year (every six months). The EB will be responsible for the day-to-day management of the project and will meet every month. The EB will be composed of the Project Coordinator and the Work Package Leaders.
