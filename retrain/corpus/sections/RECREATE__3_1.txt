3.1  Work plan and resources   
In line with the scientific and technological objectives of RECREATE, the work in this 48-month project has been 
structured in 9 work packages (WP, Fig.16), each sub-divided into a number of dedicated tasks covering the activities 
planned for the project, as listed in detail in Table 4. The project coordinator will be Prof. G. Griffini, associate 
professor in Materials Science and Technology at POLIMI. Each WP will be led and managed by a project partner 
who is expert in the relevant field, as described in detail in Section 3.2. This WP structure aims to set the basis for a 
rational and seamless execution of project acti vities and successfully achieve the foreseen objectives, implemented 
in a reasonable and cost -effective manner. The scientific, technological, techno -economical, environmental and 
educational challenges addressed in RECREATE are in line with the HORIZON -CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01-01 
topic. Active collaboration between all 21 partners will generate new scientific knowledge, develop novel, sustainable 
and economically profitable technologies, implement novel approaches to learning and education in the field of 
composite circularity.
