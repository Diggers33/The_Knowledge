Excellence #@REL-EVA-RE@#  Motivation and Background The concept of food systems has emerged over the past few years, encompassing “all the elements (environment, people, inputs, processes, infrastructures, institutions, etc.) and activities that relate to the production, processing, distribution, preparation and consumption of food, and the output of these activities, including socio-economic and environmental outcomes” (HLPE 20171). Those activities are undertaken by food systems stakeholders, are steered by various drivers (e.g. environmental, social, policy, economic, technological etc) and are influenced by stresses and shocks.
