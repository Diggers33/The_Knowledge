Excellence 4.1.1 Objectives (up to 2000 characters) The overall objective of the PLAST2bCLEANED project is to develop a system capable of real-time in-line sorting of plastic waste streams to boost the circularity of plastics in Europe. To achieve this goal, we will develop a system based on hyperspectral imaging (HSI) to increase the speed and sorting accuracy in recycling facilities for plastic waste. The development of this system will allow us to achieve the following specific objectives (O): O1. To develop a hyperspectral camera that allows the identification of different types of polymers, additives, and pigments in real-time. O2. To develop software to convert the raw data from the HSI camera into meaningful information for the sorting process. O3. To develop robust algorithms for the identification of polymers, contaminants, and additives based on the hyperspectral data. O4. To develop a system for automatic in-line sorting with significantly higher speed and accuracy than current systems. O5. To demonstrate the developed system in a real environment (advanced recycling facility for plastic waste) and validate the system with real waste streams (mixed waste, post-industrial, post-consumer, and multi-layer films). O6. To evaluate the circularity of the output products of the sorting system. O7.
