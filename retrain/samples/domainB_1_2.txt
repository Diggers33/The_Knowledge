1.3 Relation to the work programme (WP)  
We will address the topic of the call (DT-FOF-01-2020) and contribute to the specific challenge identified in it.  
We will do so by addressing all the bullet points listed in the scope of the call:  
1. The main challenges related to the development of the tool are:  
- Design of a sensory system that can be non-intrusively integrated to the manufacturing line and that can be easily connected to the automation system of a manufacturing line.  
- Design and development of the sensory system to be as flexible as possible to be able to be used in different manufacturing lines.  
- The tool should be able to be connected to the digital twin of the process and to the automation system of the plant and adapt accordingly.  
- Provide real-time information on the state of the process, and on the product quality and properties.  
- Design of a sensory system that can be easily calibrated and can be used in different manufacturing lines and for different products.
