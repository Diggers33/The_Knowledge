=== Domain C: hyperspectral imaging for automated plastic waste sorting in circular economy facilities ===
=== Section 2_1: Expected outcomes and impacts ===

1.1.1 We will build on our expertise in hyperspectral imaging, optical sensing, and machine learning to develop an innovative application for the circular economy, with a focus on recycling. We will use our technical expertise to build an integrated system that enables smart, automated plastic sorting in recycling facilities. This will be achieved through the following tasks:

Task 1.1: We will develop a hyperspectral imaging system for automated plastic waste sorting in circular economy facilities. This system will be based on a custom-designed hyperspectral camera, integrated with the appropriate illumination and acquisition system. The system will be capable of capturing spectral information across a wide range of wavelengths to identify different types of plastics accurately. We will develop data processing strategies, including machine learning algorithms, to analyze hyperspectral images and classify plastic waste materials with high precision. This task will result in a prototype hyperspectral imaging system that can be integrated into recycling processes for efficient plastic waste sorting.
