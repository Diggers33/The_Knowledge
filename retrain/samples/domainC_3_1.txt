=== Domain C: hyperspectral imaging for automated plastic waste sorting in circular economy facilities ===
=== Section 3_1: Work plan and work packages ===

3.1 Work plan — Work packages, deliverables
Work package number 1 Start month 1
Work package title Design and development of a novel hyperspectral imaging system
Activity type RTD
Participant number 1 2 3 4 5 6
Short name IRIS TUD TU FZJ CNR AVT
Person-months per participant 36 30 30 30 30 30
Objectives
• Development of a novel hyperspectral imaging system to enable an accurate and automated sorting of PET, PE, PP, PS, and PVC plastics in circular economy facilities
• Development of a novel hyperspectral image analysis method for automated, accurate, and fast classification of plastic waste
• Development of an innovative imaging system to be embedded in the sorting facility for real-time sorting of waste plastics to enable the circular economy
Description of work
1.1 Development of a novel hyperspectral imaging system. IRIS will take the lead in developing a novel hyperspectral imaging system, based on NIR spectroscopy, to enable an accurate and automated sorting of PET, PE, PP, PS, and PVC plastics in circular economy facilities. The system will be developed to identify the different plastics’ types and will be embedded in the sorting facility for real-time sorting of plastic waste. The system will use a push-broom scanning method.
1.2 Development of an image analysis method for automated classification of plastic waste. The IRIS team will develop a novel image analysis method that will enable an automated, accurate, and fast classification of plastic waste. The method will be based on a deep-learning approach. The developed method will be tested on the hyperspectral images acquired in 1.1.
1.3 Development of a novel imaging system to be embedded in the sorting facility. The imaging system will be embedded in the sorting facility to enable real-time sorting of waste plastics and to enable the circular economy.
Deliverables
D1.1: Prototype of the hyperspectral imaging system D1.2: Image analysis method for automated classification of plastic waste D1.3: Imaging system to be embedded in the sorting facility
Milestones
M1.1: Design of the imaging system M1.2: Development of the image analysis method M1.3: Prototype of the hyperspectral imaging system M1.4: Imaging system embedded in the sorting facility
